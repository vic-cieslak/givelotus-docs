{{< /blocks/cover>}} {{% blocks/lead color="dark" type="section" %}} {{< youtube ujCfPhGrQ5A >}} Lotus is a priceless digital token of appreciation that you can always keep at hand {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{% blocks/section color="light" type="section" width="sm" %}}

Lotus is a token used to show appreciation for positive action. Most digital tokens are rewarded only to the developers or miners. With Lotus, new tokens are given directly to self-organizing groups of individuals working towards causes which benefit all of humanity -- ones with which you can get involved. When you give or accept Lotus, are supporting the commons and adding value to the worldwide economy.

The initiatives you support when you use Lotus are:

  • Open Source Software
  • Digital Privacy
  • Humanitarian Efforts
  • Permaculture
  • Renewable Power
  • Human Rights
